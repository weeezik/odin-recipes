Healthy Taco Salad

Our good-looking salad!

Any time I make tacos for dinner, I usually have a taco salad for lunch the next day to use up any leftover ingredients. But today’s healthy taco salad recipe isn’t just any kitchen sink salad. What’s special about this taco salad is a yummy veggie “taco meat.”

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Taco "Meat"
  • Pico de Gallo
  • Tortilla chips
  • Red cabbage and radishes
  • Jalapeno
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F
  2. Make taco "meat"
  3. Assemble the salad
